About Our Institute

Empowering the innovators of tomorrow through STEM excellence.

Our Story

Leading the Way in Tech Education

The Robotics & Coding Institute is a premier educational center dedicated to providing world-class training in robotics, artificial intelligence, and programming. We cater to learners of all ages, from curious children to ambitious professionals.

Our curriculum is designed by industry experts to ensure that our students gain practical, hands-on experience that translates directly to real-world applications.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, accessible STEM education that empowers individuals to become creators and problem-solvers in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in robotics education, fostering a community of innovators who use technology to build a better, more sustainable future for all.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you cater to?
We have programs for kids (ages 7-12), teens (ages 13-17), and adults (18+). Each course is tailored to the specific developmental stage of the learner.
Do I need prior experience to join?
No prior experience is required for our beginner courses. We start from the basics and gradually move to advanced concepts.
Are the classes online or in-person?
We offer both in-person classes at our Nairobi campus and interactive online sessions for students who cannot attend physically.
Do you provide certificates?
Yes, all students receive a certificate of completion upon successfully finishing a course and its associated projects.
